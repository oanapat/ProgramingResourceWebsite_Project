Welcome to Programming: it's a girl thing!

Why this website?

After graduating with a BA in Political Science and a Masters Degree in International Law I found myself looking for a job for 6 months. The position that I found was as a game tester and German localizer.

This was my first introduction to the IT world. Because I was curious how some things work, I started with codeacademy, went to some events and decided to go to school for an AA in programming.

This website is meant to be a resource for girls and women.

Coding for Girls

If you want to change the world and help communities develop, invent new things that make a difference in people's lives, this is the field to be in.Programming offers such a rewarding career: you can use your imagination, creativity, humour, problem solving skills to have an impact in people's lives.

Coding for ladies

I never imagined that I would change my career but here I am. It is a scary process, but a rewarding one. You can bring with you all your background, adapt it and give everything a new meaning. Programming is about empowerment, learning new skills and changing the world. Go for it!
