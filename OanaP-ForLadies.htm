Where to start

Ada Academy

Ada Developers Academy (Ada) is an intensive software developer training school for women. Ada's unique year-long program begins with six months of classroom instruction in cutting-edge web technologies in which students learn the most up-to-date development skills from leading regional and national experts.

Rails girls

Rails girls aims to give tools and provide a community for women to understand technology and to build their ideas. Rails girls does this by providing a great experience on building things and by making technology more approachable. Learn sketching, prototyping, basic programming and get introduced to the world of technology.

Girl develop it

Code can be the most creative tool in your toolbox. Meet the full-time dreamers, beautiful-makers, and culture hackers whose code is changing the world.
